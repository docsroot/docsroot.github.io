What’s the difference between digitization, digitalization, and digital transformation?

1/ Digitization

We convert from using physical documents (paper) into files, then storing and sharing them is faster and easier.

2/ Digitalization

Create new type of records so accessing information is faster and better. Data is not in cabinet or in files. It's instantly accessibile.

=> Create new applications for business's need.

3/ Digital transformation
